About Us

We have been providing quality sands to fellow americans since the founding of our company in 1833.
All of our sand is ethically sourced from the Snake Pits of the Great Basin Desert, surrounding our company headquarters in Utah.

Our Founder

Our founder, Nathaniel Dustins, founded Sand Land after discovering the Snake Pits in Utah more than a decade before it became
an official state of the U.S. Originally, the goal of the company was to capture and sell snakes, but after the Snake Oil Distribution Act of 1852
Sand Land shifted towards more fruitful ventures: sand.
