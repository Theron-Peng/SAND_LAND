Tours

We offer tours of the Snake Pits in the months from September to February, outside of the snakes' active seasons. All tourists must follow the safety guidelines:

  • Photography is not allowed
  • Do not feed the snakes
  • Wear protective gear (provided)
  • Remain on trails at all times
